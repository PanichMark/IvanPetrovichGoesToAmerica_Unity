Open the weapon selection wheel for the right hand by holding the "Q" key, for the left hand – the "E" key.
To equip a weapon, click on its icon.
When hovering over firearms, the number of rounds in the magazine and in reserve will be displayed.
You can also replenish health or mana by clicking on the shawarma or cough syrup button in the bottom-left corner of the weapon selection wheel menu.
Attack with the weapon in the right hand – "Right Mouse Button", in the left hand - "Left Mouse Button".
