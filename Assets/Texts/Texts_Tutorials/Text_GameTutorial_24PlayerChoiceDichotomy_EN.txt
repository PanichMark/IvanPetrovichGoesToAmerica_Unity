Playing as Ivan Petrovich, you can act in the game in 2 ways:
1)Using the Categorical Imperative (act as you would want all others to act), meaning do not kill anyone and do not steal stuff. In this case, you will get the good ending of the video game.
2)Applying Resentment (resentment towards others' success and the unfairness of the world), meaning kill people, rob them of money, and commit other bad deeds. In this case, you will get the bad ending of the video game.
