You have received a special weapon – the Firefighter's Plunging Crossbow.
This weapon does not spend ammo or mana, nor does it deal damage.
But the Firefighter's Plunging Crossbow allows you to grapple onto surfaces within its usage distance.
This weapon allows you to reach places where you simply cannot just climb or gain access.
Using the Firefighter's Plunging Crossbow, you can also pull enemies and movable objects towards you from afar.
