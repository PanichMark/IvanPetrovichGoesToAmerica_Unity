Demo version of the video game "Ivan Petrovich Goes to America" was developed for Personal computers for it to be played. The developer, Mark Panich Vladislavovich, tried his best to make it interesting both for those who have never played on a computer before and for experienced gamers.
As you progress through certain stages, tutorial hints will be shown to you, explaining how specific aspects of the game work.
If you don't want to see such tutorials and prefer to figure things out yourself, you can disable them in the game settings (the settings also have many other functions, accessible from the pause menu – press the "ESC" button).
You can also remind yourself of any hints at any time in the "Tutorials" section, available in the pause menu.
