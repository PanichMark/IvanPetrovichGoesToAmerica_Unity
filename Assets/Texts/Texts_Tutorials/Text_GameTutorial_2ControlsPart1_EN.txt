The game implies control via keyboard and mouse (gamepad support is currently absent).
The following controls are listed as default buttons; if they don't work, it means they were rebinded in the game's control settings. You can reset them there to default or rebind them to your liking.
Move with "W", "A", "S", "D" keys and control the camera with the mouse.
To run, hold down the "LeftShift" key.
