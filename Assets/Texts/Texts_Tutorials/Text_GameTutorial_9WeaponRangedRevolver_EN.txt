You have received your very first firearm – the Revolver.
This is a semi-automatic weapon with slight recoil and medium damage. Effective at almost any range.
The Revolver uses 9mm ammo; when using firearms, the number of bullets in the magazine and reserve is displayed at the bottom of the screen, next to the active hand.
Reload firearms by pressing the "R" key.
Aim for the enemies' heads! This way firearms will deal much more damage!
